4
What is the purpose of this service?

5
How does a smart-contract work?

When a seller and a buyer agree on a transaction, they can use smart-contracts, which provide security for both buyer and seller. When the buyer/seller sends their funds into a smart-contract they wait for confirmation from the second party. The second party confirms and sends the goods to the buyer/seller. The buyer/seller confirms that he/she has received the goods. When all confirmations are done, the smart-contract automatically sends the funds to the second party.

6
Can a smart-contract go wrong?

No, a smart contract has a clear algorithm and parameters that cannot be violated.

7
What should I do if my funds have not arrived?
